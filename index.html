About this view

Sunrise Ridge Lookout delivers a sweeping perspective of the Cascade volcanic arc. The vantage point lines up perfectly with morning light as it breaks over the eastern ridge, creating dramatic shadows across the glacial valley. Nearby alpine pines frame the scene and provide shelter from prevailing winds.

Alex captured both still imagery and a short drone pass to document the ridge crest. Seasonal access varies; check the latest ranger advisories before heading out.
